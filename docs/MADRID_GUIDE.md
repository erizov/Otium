# Madrid city guide

Same model as `barcelona/`: English titles (`name_en`), Spanish subtitle
(`subtitle_es`), city coat of arms and **Community of Madrid** flag on the
title page, ~20 Commons-backed places (Golden Triangle museums, Habsburg core,
Retiro, Chamartín football).

- **Scripts:** `download_madrid_images.py`, `validate_madrid_sources.py`,
  `build_madrid_pdf.py` (`--html-only` for HTML without Playwright PDF).
- **Images:** committed under `madrid/images/` for reproducibility.
